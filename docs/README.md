# Documentation

This folder contains executive-facing materials that translate analytical
findings into clear, actionable HR strategy.

The documents here are designed for **senior HR, Talent, and L&D leaders**
and focus on decision support rather than technical detail.

---

## Contents

### HR_Executive_Overview.pdf
Concise executive deck summarizing:
- The internal mobility problem statement
- Data sources and analytical approach (high level)
- Key mobility insights that consistently appear across analyses
- Rule-based mobility personas and their business implications
- Strategic risk segments for targeted intervention
- HR strategy recommendations and next steps

This deck is intended to support leadership conversations about:
- Where to **increase** internal mobility investment
- Where to **maintain** existing programs
- Where to **avoid over-investment** or redesign efforts

---

## Intended Audience

- HR Leadership
- Talent Management & L&D Leaders
- People Analytics & Workforce Planning Partners

---

## Design Principles

- Clear, non-technical language
- Focus on population-level insights
- Explicit linkage between data, insight, and action
- Ethical, explainable use of workforce data

---

## Notes

- All insights are based on anonymized, aggregated data
- Personas are descriptive and should not be used for individual decisions
- Recommendations are directional and intended to guide strategy, not policy
